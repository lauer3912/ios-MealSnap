# MealSnap - AI Nutrition & Meal Tracking

## App Description

**MealSnap** is your smart nutrition companion. Snap photos of your meals, track calories and macros, discover healthy recipes, and achieve your wellness goals with AI-powered insights.

## Key Features (50+)

### Food Logging
- 📸 **Photo Food Logging** - Snap a photo to log your meal instantly
- 🔍 **Food Search** - Search our database of 100,000+ foods
- 📊 **Barcode Scanner** - Scan product barcodes for instant nutrition info
- 🍽️ **Meal Categories** - Organize by Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks
- 📝 **Quick Add** - Manually enter nutrition data

### AI Nutrition Analysis
- 🧠 **Smart Calculations** - Automatic calorie and macro computation
- 🎯 **Personalized Goals** - Set targets based on your profile
- 📈 **Progress Tracking** - Visual charts of your daily/weekly intake
- 🔄 **Macro Distribution** - Protein, Carbs, Fat balance insights

### Meal Planning
- 📅 **Meal Plans** - Weekly customizable meal plans
- 🍳 **Recipe Ideas** - 100+ healthy recipes with full nutrition
- 🛒 **Shopping Lists** - Auto-generate grocery lists from meal plans
- 🔄 **Meal Prep Tips** - Save time with batch cooking suggestions

### Premium Features ($2.99/month)
- 💎 **AI Food Analysis** - Advanced photo-based nutrition detection
- 📊 **Deep Analytics** - Weekly/monthly nutrition reports
- 🎯 **Personalized Plans** - AI-generated meal plans for your goals
- 📱 **Ad-Free Experience** - Clean, distraction-free interface
