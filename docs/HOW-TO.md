# MealSnap User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Set Your Goals
When you first open MealSnap, set your daily calorie and macronutrient goals based on your fitness objectives:
- **Weight Loss**: Reduce calorie target by 500 cal/day
- **Maintenance**: Use your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure)
- **Muscle Gain**: Add 250-500 cal/day to your TDEE

### 2. Log Your First Meal

#### Using Photo:
1. Tap the **+** button on the Dashboard
2. Select **"Take Photo"**
3. Snap a clear photo of your meal
4. The app will analyze and estimate nutrition

#### Using Search:
1. Go to the **Search** tab
2. Type your food (e.g., "grilled chicken breast")
3. Tap the result to add it
4. Adjust serving size if needed

#### Quick Add:
1. Tap the **+** button
2. Select **"Quick Add"**
3. Enter food name and nutrition values manually

### 3. Track Your Progress

View your daily progress on the Dashboard:
- **Calorie Ring** - Shows calories consumed vs. goal
- **Macro Cards** - Protein, Carbs, Fat progress bars
- **Weekly Chart** - 7-day calorie trend

## Features Guide

### Meal Types
Log foods under these categories:
- 🌅 **Breakfast** - Morning meals
- ☀️ **Lunch** - Midday meals
- 🌙 **Dinner** - Evening meals
- 🍎 **Snacks** - Between-meal bites

### Recipes
Browse healthy recipes:
1. Go to **Recipes** tab
2. Filter by favorites or quick meals (<15 min)
3. Tap a recipe for full instructions
4. Save to your meal plan

### Barcode Scanner
Scan packaged food barcodes:
1. Tap **Search** → **Scan Barcode**
2. Point camera at barcode
3. Select product from results
4. Add to your log

### Weekly Reports
View your nutrition summary:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Weekly Report**
2. See average calories and macros
3. Track trends over time
4. Identify best/worst days

## Settings

### Adjust Goals
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Daily Goals**
3. Update calories and macros
4. Changes apply immediately

### Dark Mode
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Toggle **Dark Mode**
3. Instant theme switch

### Notifications
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Enable **Meal Reminders**
3. Set reminder times for each meal

## Data Export

Export your nutrition history:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Export Data**
3. Choose format (CSV)
4. Share via email or save to Files

## Troubleshooting

### Food Not Found
Try:
- Different search terms
- Check spelling
- Use Quick Add to enter manually

### Photo Analysis Issues
Ensure:
- Good lighting on food
- Clear, unobstructed photo
- Food is the main subject

### App Running Slow
Try:
- Close background apps
- Restart your device
- Update to latest iOS

## Tips & Tricks

### Quick Logging
- Use **Favorites** to save frequent foods
- Enable **Quick Add** for fastest entry
- Set **default serving sizes**

### Stay on Track
- Enable meal reminders
- Review weekly reports
- Adjust goals as you progress

### Meal Prep
- Plan meals on Sunday
- Use recipes with overlapping ingredients
- Batch cook proteins in advance
