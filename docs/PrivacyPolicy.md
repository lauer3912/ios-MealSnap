# Privacy Policy

**MealSnap** respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

## Information We Collect

**Food & Nutrition Data**
- Food items you log (name, calories, macros, serving size)
- Meal photos (stored locally on your device)
- Barcode scan data

**Health & Fitness Goals**
- Your daily calorie and macronutrient goals
- Meal preferences and dietary restrictions

**App Usage Data**
- Session duration and feature usage
- Crash reports and performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide personalized nutrition tracking
- Generate meal plans and recipes
- Calculate daily nutrition summaries
- Send meal reminders (optional)

## Data Storage

- All nutrition data is stored **locally on your device**
- Photos are stored in the app's private directory
- We do **not** sell or share your data with third parties

## Permissions Requested

- **Camera**: To take photos of your meals
- **Photo Library**: To save and load meal photos
- **Notifications**: To send meal reminders (optional)

## Subscription

MealSnap offers a Premium subscription:
- $9.99 one-time base purchase
- $2.99/month for Premium features

All payments are processed by Apple.

## Your Rights

- Access all your data through the app
- Export your nutrition history
- Delete your data at any time
- Opt out of notifications

## Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at: **dev@ggsheng.com**

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the App Store listing.
